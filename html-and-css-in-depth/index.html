Welcome to Mangata and Gallo

Mangata and Gallo specializes in jewelry for special occasions like engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Our selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look. We are based in Austin, Texas and have a small storefront with an atelier for browsing.

Rings

At Mangata and Gallo, we offer a variety of unique and high-quality rings for men and women. Our selection includes diamond rings, platinum bands, and custom designs. We also carry a wide range of engagement rings from leading designers.

Earrings

Mangata and Gallo offers a range of stylish and trendy earrings. From classic hoop earrings to bold stud sets, we have the perfect piece for any occasion. Our selection includes diamonds, gold, and silver.
